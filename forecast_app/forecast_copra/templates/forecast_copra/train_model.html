How to read this:

Model Parameter Identification (ACF & PACF)

Use these plots to make an initial guess for p and q, then train several models with nearby (p,d,q) values and compare validation metrics.

How to Identify Parameters:
  • AR (p): Look at PACF. The lag where it cuts off (enters blue zone) suggests p.
  • MA (q): Look at ACF. The lag where it cuts off suggests q.
  • Differencing (d): If ACF decays very slowly, try higher d values (1 or 2).
💡 Best Practice: Try 3-5 different (p,d,q) combinations near your ACF/PACF suggestions. Compare their validation MAPE - pick the model with lowest validation error and reasonable test error.
